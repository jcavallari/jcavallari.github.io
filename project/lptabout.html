Who are Limited Play Time?

Like many adults in the board game hobby, we’re busy people with limited time. So we created Limited Play Time to discuss the games we’ve had time to try, but often don’t have time to do deep dives or dozens of repeated plays of, in a short enough time that you could listen to an episode in one day’s full commute to and from work.

Jason enjoys history lessons that he teaches to others, cybernetic implants, time-travel, and plastic miniatures.

Kyle enjoys little wooden cubes, hexagons, skulls colored red (but not The Red Skull), and neoprene.
